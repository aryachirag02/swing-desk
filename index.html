What the labels mean (plain English)
Score (0–100)
One number combining trend, momentum, strength vs the market, volume and volatility. Higher = healthier setup.
Signal states
Strong Buy score ≥ 80 + entry trigger fired · Buy ≥ 70 + trigger · Watchlist ≥ 60, no trigger yet — stalk it.
Hold / Reduce / Exit — stocks you own
Anything in your Trade log automatically switches to these on this table, using your entry price. Exit: price at/below your 2×ATR stop, score < 40, or ~6 weeks with no progress. Reduce: +2R reached (book half) or score fading below 50. The reason shows under the chip.
Entry trigger
What actually gets you in: a breakout to a new 20-day high on ≥1.4× normal volume, or a pullback bounce off the rising 20-day EMA. A high score without a trigger = Watchlist, not Buy.
Trend
Up ↑ = price above the 50-day average and rising. Only buy Up-trends.
Momentum (RSI)
Strong = the sweet spot (55–70). Overheated (>70) = late to chase. Weak (<45) = leave it.
vs Nifty
How much the stock beat/lagged the index over 1 month. Winners tend to keep winning over a 4–6 week horizon.
Volume
Surge = 1.5× its normal volume — big money confirming the move.
Trade plan
Entry ≈ last close · Stop = entry − 2×ATR (2 normal daily wiggles) · Target = 2R. Risk/share = entry − stop → position size = (1% of capital) ÷ risk per share.
Regime lamps
Whole-market health from Nifty vs its 50 & 200-day averages. Green: full size · Yellow: half size · Red: no new buys.
Universe & size filter
Nifty 500 = Nifty 100 large caps + Midcap 150 + Smallcap 250 — so all midcaps are in by construction. Use the size dropdown to trade only the segment you want (midcaps are the classic swing sweet spot; smallcaps are the riskiest).
Market breadth
% of the whole universe above its 50-day average, plus today's advances vs declines. Broad = healthy rally · Narrow = a few heavyweights carrying the index, be selective.
📅 results flag
Company reports results inside your holding window (next ~30 days) — earnings gaps can jump right past a stop-loss, so size down or wait.
ASM / GSM flag
Stock is on the exchange's surveillance list (unusual price/volume activity). Auto-blocked from Buy signals no matter the score.
